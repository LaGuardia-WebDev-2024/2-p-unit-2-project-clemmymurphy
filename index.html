About the Artist:

Clementine Murphy is a 17 year old artist living in brooklyn.Clementine has a heavy focus on fashion art with multiple constucted garments, exspiremental drape work and textile exspirementation present throughout her work. She is inspired by clubkid culture in the 80's aswell as brutalist architecture from around the world. She hopes to continue her studies at the prestigious Central Saint Martins with Influencial Alumni like Alexander Mqueen and Raf simons.
